Fact 2: The project was launched by Coinbase, making it one of the most heavily regulated L2 networks.
Fact 3: Base does not have its own token (gas fees are paid in ETH), which reduces regulatory risks.
Fact 4: Base’s primary mission is to bring the next billion users into crypto (Onchain is the next online).

Fact 6: The Farcaster protocol has been launched on Base, becoming the leading decentralized social network of 2024–2025.
Fact 7: Fees on the Base network have dropped by a factor of ten following the Ethereum Dencun update (EIP-4844).
Fact 8: The Base Ecosystem Fund incubator supports young projects in their early stages.
Fact 9: Base uses Optimistic Rollups technology to ensure security through the main Ethereum network.
Fact 10: In 2025, Base became the leader among L2s in terms of the number of active wallets and daily transactions.
Fact 11: Base aims to bring the next billion users on-chain by providing a secure, low-cost, and developer-friendly environment.
Fact 12: Base is built on the OP Stack in collaboration with Optimism, making it part of the Superchain ecosystem.
Fact 13: LayerZero is an omnichain interoperability protocol that allows dApps to build across multiple blockchains.
